SEASONAL EVENTS

Throughout the year, some special events and seasonal events are held in the Rexburg area. In this website we will keep you updated with those events, when they are held, how to get to their locations and the cost (if any). Our purpose is to help you find fun activities to have a great time even if you only have a free afternoon and not the whole day to drive to more distant places.

4th of July Parade

Date/Time: Every 4th of July @ 10:00 am

Location: Starting @ Smith Park, 3rd East Main St. Rexburg, Idaho

Every year the town of Rexburg organizes a parade to celebrate Independence Day. It is such a fun thing to do to celebrate to our country with friends and family.

Halloween Party

Date/Time: October 27th 5:00pm - 8:00pm

Location: 160 W 2nd S, Rexburg, Idaho

Hemming Village is in charge of this fun event. Every Halloween season, they decorate their whole street and have different activities to get the most out of this spooky season.
